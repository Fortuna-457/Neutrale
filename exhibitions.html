EXHIBITIONS

Stop, feel, think & react

OUR GOAL

Make people stop, feel, think and act

On 3 December 1955, the first annual exhibition ‘Neutrale Photo 55’ opened its doors. Believing in the power of showing and the importance of seeing high-quality visual stories, we have since organized nearly 3000 exhibitions.

Presenting the results of the foundation’s annual contests, the annual Neutrale Photo Exhibition showcases the best photojournalism and documentary photography of the last year. We also specially curate exhibitions and projects, working with themes in our archive.

NEUTRALE PHOTO 2023

Neutrale Photo Exhibition 2023

Selected by an independent jury from more than 60,000 entries by 3,752 photographers from 127 countries, the Neutrale Photo Exhibition 2023 presents the winning images from the 66th annual Neutrale Photo Contest.

The Neutrale Photo Exhibition 2023 will premiere at De Nieuwe Kerk in Amsterdam, the Netherlands, on 22 April before starting its worldwide tour to over 60 cities around the world.
