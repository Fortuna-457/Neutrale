About Us

A neutral and independent newspaper

"Journalism is free or is it a farce."
- Rodolfo Walsh -

For more than 50 years, Neutrale has been working as an independent non-profit organization.

In that time, the world has continually changed, and new advances in media and technology have transformed journalism and storytelling.

Our mission has expanded, and we draw on our experience to guide visual journalists, storytellers and audiences around the world through this challenging and exciting landscape.

Our Story

Rodolfo Walsh, our founder

Rodolfo Jorge Walsh Gill was an Argentine journalist and writer, recognized for being a pioneer in the writing of testimonial novels such as "Operación Masacre" and "¿Quién mató a Rosendo?".

On March 25, 1977, he was kidnapped by an ESMA Task Force and his name was added to the tragic list of the disappeared of the Argentine dictatorship.
